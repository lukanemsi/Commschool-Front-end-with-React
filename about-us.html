About Company

Our History

Microsoft was founded in 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen in Redmond, Washington. Since its inception, the company has played a pivotal role in the evolution of the technology industry, introducing groundbreaking products and shaping the way we interact with computers.

Our Mission

Microsoft's mission is to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more. We are committed to creating innovative technologies that enhance productivity, foster creativity, and drive positive change in the world.

Innovating the Future: Microsoft's Vision

In a strategic shift, Microsoft is steering towards a future dominated by advancements in artificial intelligence (AI) and software engineering. With a focus on innovation, the tech giant aims to reshape industries by developing cutting-edge AI solutions and elevating the field of software engineering. Simultaneously, Microsoft is nurturing revolutionary startups, providing resources and mentorship to drive disruptive changes across diverse sectors. This marks a visionary journey where Microsoft, committed to empowering individuals and organizations, plays a pivotal role in shaping a world where technology becomes a force for positive transformation.
